Asthma

Overview

A chronic respiratory condition causing airway inflammation. Effectively managed with proper treatment.

Symptoms

  • Difficulty Breathing
  • Wheezing or Whistling Sound
  • Frequent Coughing Fits
  • Chest Tightness or Pain

Causes and Care

Common Causes

  • Allergens (pollen, dust, pet dander)
  • Smoke and air pollution
  • Colds, flu, or sinus infections
  • Exercise or physical activity

Your Treatment Plan

  • Personalized treatment plan from a licensed provider
  • Prescription for quick-relief (rescue) inhalers
  • Prescription for long-term control medications
  • Expert guidance on identifying and avoiding triggers
