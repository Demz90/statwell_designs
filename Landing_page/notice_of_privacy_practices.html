Notice of Privacy Practices

How We Protect and Use Your Health Information

Your Rights

  • Access your medical records.
  • Request an amendment if the information is incorrect.
  • Request restrictions on disclosures.
  • Receive an accounting of disclosures.
  • File a complaint with the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) if you believe your privacy rights have been violated.

How We Use Your Information

Treatment, Payment, and Healthcare Operations

We use and disclose your health information for treatment, payment, and healthcare operations purposes. This includes coordinating your care with other healthcare providers, processing billing and insurance claims, and conducting quality improvement activities.

With Your Authorization

For other uses not described in this notice, we will obtain your written authorization before using or disclosing your health information. You may revoke such authorization at any time in writing.

As Required by Law

We may use and disclose your health information when required by law, such as for public health activities, reporting abuse or neglect, responding to legal requests from law enforcement or courts, and complying with workers' compensation laws.

Complete HIPAA Notice

A full HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices is provided upon registration and is available at any time through your patient portal or by contacting our Privacy Officer.
