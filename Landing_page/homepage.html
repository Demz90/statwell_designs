Frequently Asked Questions

What is StatWellMD and how does it work?

+
StatWellMD is a secure, HIPAA-compliant telemedicine platform that connects you to licensed U.S. clinicians online. Simply complete a short medical questionnaire, and a provider will review your case—usually within hours. If medically appropriate, your prescription will be sent directly to your preferred pharmacy. No video calls or waiting rooms required.

What conditions can StatWellMD treat?

+
We treat many common, non-emergency conditions, such as:
• Urinary tract infections (UTIs)
• Sinus infections, allergies, and cold sores
• Acne, eczema, and other skin conditions
• Birth control, women's health, and men's health
• Anxiety, insomnia, and depression refills

Each case is reviewed by a state-licensed provider who ensures your treatment is safe and appropriate.

How much does a StatWellMD visit cost?

+
Each visit is a flat $29.99 — simple, affordable, and transparent. There are no hidden fees, and insurance is not required.

Are StatWellMD providers licensed and qualified?

+
Yes. All StatWellMD providers are U.S.-licensed physicians, nurse practitioners, or physician assistants trained in telehealth medicine. Each consultation follows evidence-based clinical protocols and state regulations to ensure safe, effective, and ethical care.

How are prescriptions sent and can I choose my pharmacy?

+
If medication is appropriate, your provider sends your prescription electronically to any pharmacy you choose. You'll receive a confirmation email once it's transmitted, and you can pick it up just like a regular prescription.

Is my information safe and confidential?

+
Absolutely. StatWellMD uses end-to-end encryption and HIPAA-compliant systems to protect your data. Your personal and medical information is never shared, sold, or disclosed—only your assigned provider and care team can access your file.

What if the provider decides I don't need medication?

+
If your provider determines medication isn't necessary, you'll receive guidance on next steps or home care. In such cases, you are not charged for a prescription. Our focus is on safe, responsible medicine, not over-prescribing.

What is the refund policy?

+
If your visit isn't approved or no prescription is issued, you receive a full refund automatically. Once a provider reviews your consultation and sends a prescription, the service is considered rendered and non-refundable per federal telehealth guidelines.

How do follow-ups or refills work?

+
Need a refill? Log into your account and submit a quick follow-up request. Many conditions qualify for discounted refill visits. Your provider may recommend periodic check-ins to ensure your treatment remains effective and safe.

Can StatWellMD prescribe controlled substances or narcotics?

+
No. StatWellMD does not prescribe controlled substances, narcotics, or high-risk medications such as Adderall, Xanax, or opioids. We specialize in non-emergency, guideline-based care that prioritizes your safety.

How quickly will I get a response from a provider?

+
Most consultations are reviewed within 2–4 hours (often faster). Requests submitted after hours are completed the next morning. You'll be notified by email and text once your provider has reviewed your case.

What states is StatWellMD available in?

+
StatWellMD currently operates in multiple U.S. states, with active expansion underway. When you begin your consultation, the platform automatically verifies if service is available in your state and notifies you as soon as new states are added.

What if there's an issue with my pharmacy or prescription?

+
If your pharmacy doesn't receive your prescription, contact our Support Team through the Help Center or by email. We'll verify and resend it—usually within an hour—so you can get your medication without delay.

Do I need insurance to use StatWellMD?

+
No insurance is needed. StatWellMD uses a simple cash-pay model that keeps care affordable and straightforward. You can still use your insurance card at the pharmacy to help cover medication costs if eligible.

Can I use StatWellMD on my phone or tablet?

+
Yes! StatWellMD is mobile-friendly and works on any device—no app required. Complete your visit, upload photos, and receive your treatment plan directly from your phone, tablet, or computer at statwellmd.com.
